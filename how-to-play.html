The Wrexham A.F.C Quiz

How to play

Step 1: To start the game press the play button


Step 2: This will then take you to you first question


Step 3: You will have a choice of easy or hard


Step 4: Once you choose your difficulty you will then begin


Step 5: Each question has a 1 minute time limit


Step 6: There is a total of 10 questions once finished you can submit you name and score
